// FAQ & GETTING STARTED

Everything a pearler needs.

New to Pearl mining or just to our pool — start here. Still stuck? The Discord has real people.

// THE BASICS

01What is Pearler Pool?
A mining pool for the Pearl (PRL) coin. You point your GPU at the pool, it combines everyone's work, and rewards are split fairly by contribution using PPLNS. Members are pearlers. No solo mining — pooled only, so payouts are smooth instead of all-or-nothing.
02What is Pearl (PRL)?
Pearl is a Layer-1 blockchain that secures itself with proof-of-useful-work: instead of throwing away hashes, miners run large matrix multiplications — the same math behind AI training and inference — and attach a zero-knowledge proof so the network can verify the work cheaply (the team calls it NoisyGEMM). Mainnet launched 27 April 2026; max supply is 2.1 billion PRL, with a new block roughly every ~3 minutes. PRL trades on SafeTrade. More in About.
03What does PPLNS mean?
Pay-Per-Last-N-Shares. When the pool finds a block, the reward is split across the most recent window of submitted shares, in proportion to how many each miner contributed. It rewards steady mining and resists reward-hopping. Our PPLNS window is read live from the pool.

// FEES & PAYOUTS

04What are the fees?
A 1% pool network fee applies to every option (read live from the pool). On top of that, your choice of miner sets a software fee: our own Pearler miner is 2%; the open-source, official, and bring-your-own options add 0% — so those pay only the 1% pool fee. The fee for each option is shown on the Mine page.
05How and when do I get paid?
Rewards accrue to your prl1… payout address as you submit shares, split via PPLNS each time the pool finds a block. Your paid and pending balances show on the Dashboard when you look up your address. Payout cadence and the minimum payout are set by the pool operator — check the Discord for the current values.
06How do I track my earnings?
Open the Dashboard and paste your prl1… address — you'll see balances, per-worker stats, a daily estimate, and a performance trend that builds from your first visit. That historical "go back in time" view is something no other Pearl pool offers.

// MINING

07What GPUs can I use?
Pearl's proof-of-useful-work needs the tensor cores in modern GPUs. In practice that's NVIDIA Turing and newer (RTX 20/30/40/50 series, plus A100/H100-class cards) and recent AMD Radeon RX 6000/7000. Pre-tensor-core cards (GTX 10-series and older) and CPU-only mining aren't supported. Rough hashrates per card are in the calculator.
08How do I start? (the easy way)
Go to the Mine page, paste your prl1… address, pick an option, and copy the Claude or Codex deploy prompt. Paste it into Claude Code, Claude in Cowork, or OpenAI Codex and the AI agent does the whole setup — checks your GPU and Docker, runs the miner, and confirms shares are landing. Prefer to do it yourself? Each option also shows a ready-to-run command.
09Can I use a miner I already have?
Yes — option 4 on the Mine page, "connect your own," gives you the stratum settings: host, TLS and plain ports, your username as <address>.<worker>, password x. No extra software fee, just the 1% pool fee.
10Windows, macOS or Linux?
Linux + NVIDIA is the smoothest path. Windows works through Docker Desktop with the WSL2 backend. On macOS, Docker can't pass through the GPU, so GPU mining with these images isn't supported there. The AI deploy prompts detect your OS and adapt.

// SAFETY

11Is it safe? Do I share any keys?
You only ever share your public payout address (prl1…) — that is not a secret and is all the pool needs. Never paste a private key or wallet seed into a miner, a prompt, or anywhere else. Our deploy prompts are written to refuse handling secrets.
12Do I need to register?
No accounts, no sign-up. Your address is your identity — start mining and look it up on the dashboard.

// TROUBLESHOOTING

13My miner runs but I see no shares.
Check the worker authorized first, then wait — the first accepted share usually lands within a minute. If you see TLS or auth errors, try the plain port instead of TLS, and confirm your username is <your-prl1-address>.<worker> with password x. Still nothing? Drop the log lines in Discord.
14Why does hashrate show "soon"?
The pool is new and the live hashrate feed isn't wired up yet, so anywhere a hashrate would appear we show "soon" rather than a fake number. Blocks, shares, balances and payouts are all live now; hashrate and effort will switch on once the feed is available.
15My address isn't on the dashboard yet.
A new address only appears after your first accepted share is recorded. Give it a few minutes of mining, then look it up again. The leaderboard and blocks lists also fill in as the pool grows.
